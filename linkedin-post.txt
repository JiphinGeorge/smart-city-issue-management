🚀 Excited to share that our team secured 2nd Prize 🥈 at the Vibe Coding Event conducted as part of TecXell 2026 at MGM Institute of Technology, Kochi!

Along with my teammate Umesh, we built a project titled "Smart City Issue Management System" — a full-stack web application designed to help citizens report, track, and manage urban issues efficiently.

🔧 What we built:

✅ AI-Powered Issue Reporting — Citizens describe issues in plain language, and the system automatically detects the category (Road, Utilities, Waste, Electric) and priority level
✅ Real-Time Dashboard — Live stats, status filters, and full-text search across all reported issues
✅ Admin Panel — A dedicated management view with tabular data, inline actions, and resolution tracking
✅ Analytics Page — Dynamic category distribution and priority breakdown charts
✅ Image Upload & Location Tagging — Attach photo evidence and geolocation data to every report
✅ Role-Based Authentication — Separate flows for Admin and Citizen users

🛠️ Tech Stack: Node.js | Express.js | TailwindCSS | Vanilla JavaScript | JSON-based data layer

The event challenged students to build innovative, real-world solutions using modern development approaches and AI-driven workflows — all within a tight time constraint. It was an incredible hands-on experience that pushed us to think fast, build smarter, and collaborate effectively.

Truly captured the spirit of hackathons — rapid ideation, clean execution, and delivering a working product under pressure 💪

A big thank you to the organizers and volunteers at MGM Institute of Technology for hosting such an amazing event 👏
